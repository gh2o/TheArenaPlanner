Welcome!
To get started:
  1. In the Courses tab, check off the courses you want to take.
  2. Rate the teachers or click the lock to lock in a certain teacher.
  3. In the Options tab, select your lunches and check off other options.
    Remember to choose your pick!
  4. Click Generate Schedules!

Good luck!
